Dating For Good

Neqtr is an invite-only relationship app for socially conscious people looking for love. We’ve made Neqtr available exclusively to those who believe in love, a healthier self, and a better world. That’s because we believe in quality over quantity when it comes to finding love, and we know you do too.

You choose dates based on your common interests. Whether it’s yoga, hiking, or rescuing stray animals, we help you make a date out of it. Because you feel your sexiest when you’re doing what you love! That’s how we make giving back sexy. Music to your ears? Apply for an invite.
