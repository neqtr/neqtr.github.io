Meet Over What Matters

Neqtr is an app for socially conscious people looking to meet others with the same passions - for love or for friendship. You match with others based on your common interests. Whether it’s yoga, fitness, activism, or rescuing stray animals, we help you find like-minded people who share your same passions. Because you shine your brightest when you’re doing what you love! That’s how we make giving back sexy.

We’ve made Neqtr available exclusively to those who believe in love, a healthier self, and a better world. That’s because we believe in quality over quantity when it comes to making connections that last, and we know you do too. Music to your ears? Download the app.

Connect Over Passions, Not Pickup Lines

Connections built on positive shared passions are difficult to find. You have a lot to offer, and so you want more. We get that. That’s why we have created this tribe. Find a yoga buddy, a workout partner, or friends to organize an animal rights rally with. If you’re looking for love, why do dinner and a movie when you can take a hike, literally, and soak in the great outdoors. Do good for the world and for yourself, and meet amazing people like you in the process.
